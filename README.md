# Brazilian E-Commerce Data Analysis  

Dive into the vibrant world of **Brazilian e-commerce**! This repository explores the **Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Dataset**, containing **100,000+ orders from 2016–2018** across multiple marketplaces. We uncover insights on customer behavior, sales trends, top products, and regional performance to reveal the heartbeat of online shopping in Brazil.  

### Datasets 
For this analysis, we focused on five key tables from the original dataset:  
1. **Orders** – Customer orders with timestamps and status.  
2. **Order Items** – Detailed products within each order.  
3. **Products** – Product metadata, including category and price.  
4. **Customers** – Customer locations and profiles.  
5. **Sellers** – Seller locations and performance metrics.  

Full dataset available on [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/olistbr/brazilian-ecommerce).  

### Analytical Focus Areas
We explored trends and insights across several dimensions:  

1. **Top Selling Regions**  
   - **Goal**: Identify regions driving the highest revenue.  
   - **Method**: Aggregate and rank sales by state.  
   - **Impact**: Helps target marketing and logistics to high-performing areas.  

2. **Top Product Categories by Revenue**  
   - **Goal**: Discover which categories generate the most revenue.  
   - **Method**: Sum total sales per category and rank them.  
   - **Impact**: Guides promotions and inventory decisions toward profitable categories.  

3. **Top 5 Products by Revenue**  
   - **Goal**: Spotlight the five highest-earning products.  
   - **Method**: Rank products by total revenue.  
   - **Impact**: Understand consumer favorites and market trends.  

4. **Monthly Order Volume Trends**  
   - **Goal**: Analyze order fluctuations to identify peak periods.  
   - **Method**: Group orders by month and year.  
   - **Impact**: Supports accurate forecasting and optimizes marketing campaigns.  

### Why This Analysis Matters
Understanding Brazilian e-commerce trends helps sellers, marketers, and analysts:  
- Identify high-performing products and regions  
- Optimize sales strategies  
- Predict seasonal demand  
- Gain actionable insights from real-world data
